Mobs in 9 Colored Game
====

`Zombie`
* Takes 3 shots to kill.
* Have a speed in between 1 and 4.
* Is rotten.
 
`Blob`:
* Only takes one shot to kill.
* Moves with a speed between 1 and 3.
* Overall a pretty easy mob.
 
`Muzz`:
* One shot one kill.
* Have a speed of either 3, 4 or 5.
* Flies after you!
